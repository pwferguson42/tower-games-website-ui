In the world of Wryn Brigandiers band together to find treasure, adventure, and a way to connect with communities. These adventures are designed to be completed in one play session, but they can also be strung together for to create a episodic story.

Brigands!!

This sandbox adventures takes the adventures through a horrific encounter with a group of bandits. After being captured it is up to the plays to explore and find a way out of this situation. This adventure maps out an area complete with encounters. The only thing the game master needs to do is write some context and get some friends together.

Seeking our Fortune

This one night adventure is split into three acts. The players will set out from the town of Littlewood seeking their fortune. The adventure provides the game master with options to setup the story or roll from a tables to create the story. There are enemy stat blocks to build encounters and tables to build out the area around the town.
